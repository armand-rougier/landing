The project

The objective we have with our team is to share and discover the real taco and the real taquería in France. The "french taco" is the most different thing from the original mexican taco. In Mexico, taco are often eaten in the street or in little restaurants, in "taquerías" as below.

We want to create a Food Truck and a restaurant in Lille to sell some mexican food as they do in Mexico. We want our customers to live a real experience.
